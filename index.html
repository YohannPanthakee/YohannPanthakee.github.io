About Me

Welcome

Hello! My name is Yohann Panthakee, and I am an aspiring motorsports engineer with a passion for Design Engineering. Welcome to my online portfolio. This website allows me to share my work with others and keep visitors informed about my current initiatives. I appreciate you coming, and I'm excited to show you my work!

My passion for design began when I was studying for my A-levels and evolved throughout the years until I started my master's program. I've worked on various projects over this time some of which are shown in the projects section below. My understanding of approaching a complex assembly has improved with each finished project. My master's group design project required me to lead the design engineering team and all this effort prepared me for that. Designing an endurance racing bike in CATIA V5 while working with a group of 11 other engineers and exchanging requirements in a short amount of time promoted efficiency and included deliberation, planning, modification that drove a successful end product like real-world motorsport.

I am looking to break into the motorsport industry as an mechanical design engineer and eventually grow to the post of technical director.

MSc Thesis

Title: Application of Deep Learning for Motorsport Video Analysis

The idea for the project originated from Virtuosi Racing, who wanted to investigate using the onboard camera to gather valuable data. As a result, I suggested using deep learning image segmentation to localise the car laterally on the track in a manner that resembles the output of a GPS module in a given local coordinate frame. I created a deep neural network, an image dataset, unique training visualisation metrics, and a script to take a video as input and generate a graph of lateral position for the project.
